Campus Life

music room

Manthan takes pride in encouraging and inspiring children to read from a very young age. It is our strong belief that reading enhances one's life both, academically and socially. As a school, we strive to make each student develop the zeal to read and enjoy different literary works.

science lab

Our science labs allow students to gain hands-on experience of what they learn in the classroom. Throughout the course of every unit, we ensure students get an opportunity for practical work in the lab in all three sciences, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
