CHAPTER 1
I read the newspaper......
I was eating breakfast when it was 8 o’ clock. I did not think any extraordinary things would happen (it never does). But sure enough, it would be extraordinary.
I read the newspaper and found something weird. It said that there was a rumor about there being a chest. A GOLDEN CHEST to be exact. I was very excited about this and the newspaper had mentioned the location and the location was a few miles from next to the house! I started packing my stuff and left. It was going to be fun.
CHAPTER 2
An adventure......
DAY #1 After I left I was feeling good. I was stocked on supplies and I got enough food so I was ready to go. I walked a bit and was tired. I wanted to sleep. I pulled out a sleeping bag. I went in and slept.
DAY #2
Once I woke up I ate an omelette and some ham and was ready to adventure more. I walked and walked for about half a mile and it was nighttime again. I pulled out my sleeping bag and slept.
DAY #3
Once I woke up I ate my breakfast; cereal and pancakes. I was starting to feel a little nervous about this. I didn't know if I had the courage to complete this.
DAY #4
I ate breakfast and walked even more. It was very tiring. I had nearly reached 1.75 miles. It became nighttime and I slept.
DAY #5
I was close to the chest. I knew it. I wanted to find the chest. Suddenly, I saw something. It was yellow and shiny. Was it the golden chest? I rubbed my eyes and looked again. It was still there. Okay then! I guess I found it on the 5th day! That was a very short time. I was very tired so I went to bed.
DAY #6
I had found the golden chest but today it wasn’t there! I was very confused but then I saw something. Someone was spying on me! I saw eyes looking directly at me. I pretended to not notice it and walked past the bush he was hiding in. I pulled out a sword that I had packed. The thief was not even prepared. He ran away with the chest. I ran faster than him and snatched the chest out of his hand. I ran with my stuff all the way back home.
I didn’t care how tired I was. I just ran and ran till I came home.
CHAPTER 3
Back Home......
As soon as I went back I put the golden chest down and tried to open it. The chest wasn’t budging! Then I noticed that there was a keyhole. Was I going to have to get a key as well? I guess I do!
