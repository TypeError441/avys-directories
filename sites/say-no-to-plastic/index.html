Over 300 million tons of plastic are produced every year and at least 8 million tons of plastic end up in the ocean every year. Since the 1950s, around 8.3 billion tons of plastic have been produced worldwide and 73% of beach litter worldwide is plastic. In some parts of the world, using plastic is already illegal. Worldwide, about 2 million plastic bags are used every minute and a million plastic bottles are bought around the world every minute. Plastic is killing more than 1.1 million seabirds and animals every year. Over the past 50 years, world plastic production has doubled.
